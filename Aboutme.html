About Me ....

Welcome to my portfolio !

This portfolio is a practical display of my journey in data analytics. Here you will find a selection of projects that showcase my skills in python, SQL, and Power BI. Each project illustrates how I approach data with a sharp analytical eye and solve problems to provide clear, actionable insights.

My Journey in the data analytics world began in my early teens when my mother would bring me to Coder Dojo in DCU on a Saturday morning. This was my introduction to the brilliance of computers. During my undergraduate studies at DCU I came to understand the significance and enormous potential of data analytics in business. This ignited my passion for this field, inspiring me to specialise in the subject in final year.

As an undergraduate I immerse myself in relevant coursework, including creating a Professional Data Analytics Portfolio, which showcases my skills in Python, SQL, Power BI, statistical analysis, data visualisations and predictive modelling.

My enthusiasm for data analytics was further nurtured during my internship at Johnson & Johnson, where I worked on projects that involved extracting valuable insights from large datasets and presenting these findings in a visually appealing way. I created multiple reports each month including the Daily Sales Report, Mass Sales Report, T&R report and any field sales reports that the pharmacy sales reps requested. I used many types of software such as BI, SAP and Dunhumby. The opportubity to apply my skills to solve tangible business problems reinforced my commitment to pursuing a career in data analytics.
